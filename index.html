About Us

Kenya Safari is one of the greatest life changing wildlife experience beyond the positive subconscious feeling generated by the community, Trysafari is a platform that offers you quality services to access the rich wildlife and the beautiful scenery of Africa.

Park Rules/Wildlife Code: Respect the privacy of the wildlife, this is their habitat. Beware of the animals, they are wild and can be unpredictable. Don't crowd the animals or make sudden noises or movements. Do not feed the animals, it upsets their diet and leads to human dependence. Please keep quiet, noise disturbs the wildlife. All clients are required to stay in your vehicle at all times, except at designated picnic or walking areas, consult your driver/tour guide. Keep below the maximum speed limit (40 kph/25 mph). Leave no litter and never leave fires unattended or discard burning objects.
